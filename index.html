Exercise 6

Responsible AI in Learning & Research

Download Report (.docx)

Task objective

Process summary

Summarize what responsible use means to you, the risks you identified, and the concrete rules you will follow in future coursework and research.

Always verify AI-generated facts before using them in coursework.
Cite or disclose AI assistance whenever course rules require transparency.
Protect personal and sensitive data when using online AI systems.
Use AI to support learning, not replace independent thinking.
Check for bias, missing perspectives, and harmful assumptions.
Respect copyright and the original work of others.
